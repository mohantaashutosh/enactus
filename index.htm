CovRING is a hand gear that can be worn as a RING on your index finger. It prevents skin contamination of COVID-19 from various surfaces by opening doors, clicking ATM buttons, carrying bags etc. with ZERO SURFACE CONTACT. It comes with a case that attaches to your belt. It has a sanisting sponge layer which can be washed & refilled with a hand sanitiser. It has an ergonomic, comfortable and elegant design, prototyped by the students of IIT Delhi.
